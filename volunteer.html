Volunteer Program

Gwarinpa, Abuja-FCT Part Time

A.I.F is actively searching for dedicated individuals to join our team of volunteers in making a positive impact on the lives of those less fortunate. Our volunteer opportunities encompass a wide range of meaningful activities, including teaching essential life skills, professional skills, and providing support in utilities maintenance. By contributing your time and expertise, you have the chance to make a tangible difference in the lives of others and empower them for a brighter future. Join us in our mission to extend a helping hand to those in need, and together, let's build a stronger, more supportive community.

Why Volunteer With Us?:

  • Meaningful Impact:
     By volunteering with us, you will contribute to initiatives that directly impact the lives of less fortunate youths/family.
  • Personal Growth:
     Our volunteer opportunities provide a platform for personal and professional development. You will gain valuable skills, experiences, and connections that will benefit you in various aspects of your life.
  • Join a Supportive Community:
     Al-Ilm Islamic Foundation is more than just a group of volunteers; it's a community that shares a common goal. You'll meet like-minded individuals who are passionate about making a positive change.

Volunteer works:

  • Community Outreach and Education
  • Food Distribution Programs
  • Educational Support Programs
  • Islamic Counseling and Guidance
  • Orphanage Support
  • Skills Development Workshops
  • Educational Support Programs
  • Women's Empowerment Initiatives
